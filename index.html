Actor of the French underground techno scene of the 90s, smldms still feeds on these dark and industrial influences in his artwork. Self-taught, he has always practiced digital arts. Multidisciplinarity is its major asset using code, photography, video and 3D motion design.

In 2007, he was also co-founder of the very first digital art festival “Empreintes Numériques” in Toulouse.

With blockchain and Art Crypto, smldms has found a field of expression consistent with its own philosophy, respecting the triptych of Art (the work, the artist and its audience), without any intermediary.

His main fields of exploration are randomness, loops and glitch.

Patterns Over Landform

P.O.L is my genesis Blueprint on Async.art

This work is a generative art project. All the elements (the 4 columns, the dunes, the trees, the circles and the mask bands) were coded separately and randomly distributed in space on a 4K canvas in P5js. Each iteration is therefore unique and 865,810,176,000 combinations are possible for only 128 copies available.
